Sort It!

Use keys 1, 2, and 3 to sort your waste items into the Paper bin, the Compost bin, or the Plastic bin.

  • 1 = PAPER
  • 2 = COMPOST
  • 3 = PLASTIC

There will be a 10 second timer counting down as soon as the game begins. When you correctly sort a waste item, the timer will reset. In the bottom right-hand corner, a scoreboard will keep track of your points. You will receive a point for every item correctly sorted. Every 10 points, the amount of time to sort an item will drop by 1 second.
